About Rhythm & Grace

Rhythm & Grace Dance & Creative Academy was founded with a simple belief: every child and adult deserves a space to express, explore, and excel.

From classical dance to modern fitness formats, from abacus to embroidery, our programs are crafted to develop discipline, creativity, and confidence—on stage and in life.

  • Experienced, child-friendly instructors
  • Performance opportunities and annual showcases
  • Structured curriculum with progressive levels
  • Safe, air-conditioned classrooms and practice studios
